What we document and why it matters.

Every product in our catalog undergoes rigorous verification. We record supplier origins, test for contaminants, and document the scientific rationale behind each formulation. This transparency ensures you receive safe, effective supplements backed by evidence and ethical sourcing.

Identity confirmation

We verify botanical species through DNA barcoding and chemical fingerprinting, ensuring each herb matches its label claim. Certificates of analysis are stored for every batch.

Contaminant awareness

Third-party labs test for heavy metals, pesticides, and microbial contamination. We publish limits of detection and actual measured values for transparency.

Batch notes

Each production run receives a unique identifier linking to harvest date, extraction method, and storage conditions. You can trace your supplement from field to bottle.

Supplier traceability

We maintain direct relationships with foragers and farms, documenting geographic origin and harvesting practices. Ethical wildcrafting and sustainable cultivation are non-negotiable.

Protocol rationale

Every formulation is backed by peer-reviewed research. We cite studies, explain dosage selection, and outline the biological mechanisms behind each ingredient's effects.

Interaction caution

We document known contraindications, drug interactions, and potential side effects. Our database cross-references medications and health conditions to flag safety concerns proactively.

Common questions.

We've gathered answers to the most frequently asked questions about combining ancient Baltic remedies with modern biohacking practices. These responses are designed to help you navigate your wellness journey safely and thoughtfully.

Can I combine forest extracts with supplements?

In many cases, yes—traditional extracts like Chaga tinctures and birch bud preparations can complement modern supplements. However, it's essential to understand potential interactions. We recommend starting with one category at a time and monitoring how your body responds. If you're taking prescription medications or have specific health conditions, please consult with a healthcare professional before combining remedies. Our team can provide guidance on timing and dosing to minimize any potential conflicts.

How do you think about medications and interactions?

We take medication interactions very seriously. Some forest botanicals can affect how your body processes pharmaceuticals—for example, certain adaptogens may influence blood pressure or blood sugar regulation. We always recommend informing your doctor about any herbal supplements you're considering. Our product descriptions include known interaction warnings, but they're not exhaustive. When in doubt, schedule a consultation with a healthcare provider who understands both traditional remedies and modern pharmacology.

How soon should I expect changes?

This varies significantly based on what you're addressing and which products you're using. Some nootropics and biohacking devices may show effects within days—improved focus, better sleep tracking data, or enhanced recovery metrics. Traditional forest extracts often work more gradually, with noticeable shifts appearing over 2-4 weeks of consistent use. We encourage keeping a simple journal to track subtle changes in energy, mood, and physical markers. Remember that wellness is a journey, not a destination, and sustainable change takes time.

Do wearables replace intuition?

Absolutely not—wearables are tools that enhance, rather than replace, your body's wisdom. The best approach combines objective data from devices with subjective awareness of how you feel. Your HRV reading might indicate stress, but only you know if that stress is productive or harmful. We view technology as a modern extension of the intuitive practices our ancestors used—observing patterns, respecting natural rhythms, and making adjustments based on lived experience. Use the data to inform your choices, but always listen to your body first.

What is a safe starting routine?

We recommend beginning with just one or two products and establishing a baseline for at least two weeks. A gentle starting point might be a morning adaptogen tincture paired with basic sleep tracking. Avoid the temptation to implement everything at once—this makes it impossible to identify what's actually helping. Start with the lowest recommended dose, especially for concentrated extracts. Pay attention to how you feel, and increase gradually only if needed. If you experience any adverse reactions, discontinue use and consult a healthcare professional.

How do you handle sensitivities?

Individual sensitivities are common and completely valid. We provide detailed ingredient lists and sourcing information for all our products, including potential allergens and cross-contamination risks. If you have known sensitivities to specific plants or compounds, please review product details carefully or contact us before purchasing. For those with multiple chemical sensitivities, we suggest starting with single-ingredient products rather than complex formulations. We're happy to recommend alternatives or create a customized approach that respects your body's unique needs.
